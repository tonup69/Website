Graduate Students

Walt Krueger

Walt received his B.S. in Biology from Bellhaven University. His research focuses on using Drosophila melanogaster (fruit flies) to dissect the molecular underpinnings of Duplication 15q related seizures.

Email: wkrueger@uthsc.edu

Ben Geier

Ben received his B.A. in Biology from Wabash College. Ben's research focuses on using Drosophila melanogaster (fruit flies) to identify potential Dube3a substrates and their relationship to downstream molecular cascades involved in gliopathic epilepsy.

Email: bgeier@uthsc.edu

Tayler Hedgecock

Tayler received his B.S. in Biology from Southeastern Oklahoma State University. His research focuses on using dental pulp stem cells to study the molecular causes of Congenital central hypoventilation syndrome (CCHS) and other autism spectrum disorders.

Email: taylerh@uthsc.edu
